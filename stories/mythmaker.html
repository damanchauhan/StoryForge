THE MYTHMAKER

In a world built on order, a hero rose to protect it — and a monster was named to justify him.

When a faceless figure known as the Ghost shatters the nation's peace, the celebrated savior Aurelion Vex leads the hunt.

But as blood stains the streets and faith hardens into worship, the line between hero and villain begins to blur.

MYTHMAKER is a story about power, lies, and the terrifying truth behind the legends we choose to believe.
